LoadMatrix Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

LoadMatrix is a freight loading and cargo visualization application designed for operational planning and educational purposes.

1. Information Collection

LoadMatrix does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.

The application may temporarily store local configuration data on your device, including:

  • Equipment dimensions
  • Load configurations
  • Visualization preferences
  • Session settings

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

2. Analytics and Diagnostics

Future versions of LoadMatrix may include anonymous crash diagnostics or usage analytics to improve stability and performance.

If analytics are introduced, users will be informed through the application.

3. Offline Usage

LoadMatrix is designed to operate primarily offline. Some features may require internet connectivity for updates or future cloud services.

4. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect locally stored application data. However, no software system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

LoadMatrix may use third-party technologies including:

  • Three.js
  • Android WebView
  • Capacitor runtime components

These technologies have their own licenses and privacy policies.

6. User Responsibility

LoadMatrix is intended as a planning and visualization tool. Users remain responsible for validating all operational, safety, and loading decisions before real-world implementation.

7. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
